2: Workerfy Worker Account page

This is the page for the worker. To the right of this page is the create account field, this field gives individual workers the ability to create a digital card that is stored and shared on workerfy for clients to go through to choose a worker. To right is the login field. This field hepls workers to login into their cards to update them with new information, delete some information or totally delete the account.
